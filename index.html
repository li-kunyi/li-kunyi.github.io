I'm a Ph.D student at the Chair of CAMP, Technical University of Munich, supervised by Federico Tombari. My research focuses on 3D vision, with a particular emphasis on SLAM, 3D scene reconstruction and understanding. I am also collaborating with Michael Niemeyer on related projects.

I hold a Master's degree in Control Engineering from the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, where I focused on light field imaging and reconstruction. Prior to that, I got both a Bachelor of Engineering from the College of Engineering and a Bachelor of Science in EECS from Peking University in 2019.
